Bucket List #1: Grandma's Concert

Grandma's Concert
8/26/2024

In the beginning of last summer, my Grandma found out about a chorus group based out of her church known as the Treble Sounds. They are an all-female choir that only meets during the summer. At the end of the August, they perform with the all-male group, Summer Sounds, to showcase the songs they've learned over the past three months. My family attended the show, and most of the songs were gospel songs, as to be expected by a church-sanctioned event. I am sorry to admit that I did find myself beginning to nod off during the performance, but I did manage to stay awake.
