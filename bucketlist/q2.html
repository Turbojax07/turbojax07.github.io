Quarter 2 Events

Bucket List #6: Painting the Rock

10/21/2024

In late October, Blue Cheese Robotics hosted a robotics competition called "Rumble with Cheese." To spread the word about the event, we decided to paint the spirit rock outside of the school.  The photos aren't great because we painted it at night, but 5 of us helped out.  We were planning on recording a promotion video at night as well, but the camera quality wasn't great, so they had to retake it in the morning. You can check it out here: Rumble Promo.



Bucket List #7: Restarting Makerfest

11/2/2024

Makerfest is a community event where people in our area get together and share the things they've made or how to make them.  The last one was in 2019, and it was shut down due to covid.  This year, Blue Cheese Robotics helped bring it back.  I was stationed at the 3d printing table where I would tell people how 3d printers worked, and show them some of the stuff we've made.  I did my best, but I haven't actually used a 3d printer, so my explanations were very simplistic.  Overall, the event went well, and I saw groups such as the American Radio Relay League and RABID Robotics there too.



Bucket List #8: Comedy Sportz

11/2/2024

After Makerfest, we went to Comedy Sportz.  Comedy Sportz is an improv show where two teams of three compete in various games to make the audience laugh.  They will even bring up people from the audience every once in a while and have them compete or give ideas.  This was a fun trip, and I'd like to go again.  However, I don't like going up on the stage, so I may try to avoid being picked.



Bucket List #9: Christmas

12/24/2024 - 12/25/2024

Every year, my family celebrates Christmas twice.  On Christmas Eve we go to one of my grandparent's house, and on Christmas Day we go to the other grandparents house.  On both days, we play games, have dinner, and open presents.  It's a great time for everyone! This year, we had a few more guests on Christmas Eve than normal because my cousins from North Carolina came up with their grandparents.  Everyone was happy that they were there as we don't see them very often.  I got a few cool things from my grandparents, including a new phone (not an expensive one), some multitool pens, and plenty of candy.  I had a great time, and it was amazing having everyone together.



Bucket List #10: Family Photo

12/27/2024

Around Christmastime, my parents signed up for an online contest to get professional photos taken with our pet.  We ended up being one of the winners, and scheduled our photos to be taken a few days after Christmas.  Unfortunately, the photography group won't let us get digital versions of our photos (At least not without a steep price), so the best I can do is a photo my mom and I took outside while we were exploring the area.  Overall, the photo experience was interesting.  To maintain our dog's interest, the photographer would make quick noises like cats meowing or clicks and whistles.  The photos turned out well when we were shown them, but we won't get them for a while as the framing process takes some time.
