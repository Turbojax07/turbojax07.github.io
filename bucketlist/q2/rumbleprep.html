Bucket List #7: Painting the Rock

Rumble with Cheese Prep
10/21/2024

In late October, Blue Cheese Robotics hosted a robotics competition called "Rumble with Cheese." To spread the word about the event, we decided to paint the spirit rock outside of the school.  The photos aren't great because we painted it at night, but 5 of us helped out.  We were planning on recording a promotion video at night as well, but the camera quality wasn't great, so they had to retake it in the morning.
