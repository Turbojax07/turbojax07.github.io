Bucket List #6: Restarting Makerfest

Makerfest
11/2/2024

Makerfest is a community event where people in our area get together and share the things they've made or how to make them.  The last one was in 2019, and it was shut down due to covid.  This year, Blue Cheese Robotics helped bring it back.  I was stationed at the 3d printing table where I would tell people how 3d printers worked, and show them some of the stuff we've made.  I did my best, but I haven't actually used a 3d printer, so my explanations were very simplistic.  Overall, the event went well, and I saw groups such as the American Radio Relay League and RABID Robotics there too.
