Bucket List #8: Visiting Comedy Sportz

Comedy Sportz
11/2/2024

After Makerfest, we went to Comedy Sportz.  Comedy Sportz is an improv show where two teams of three compete in various games to make the audience laugh.  They will even bring up people from the audience every once in a while and have them compete or give ideas.  This was a fun trip, and I'd like to go again.  However, I don't like going up on the stage, so I may try to avoid being picked.
